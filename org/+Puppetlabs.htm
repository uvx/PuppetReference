There are many reasons why a Domain Group Policy may not be solving all your #Windows security and configuration problems. Sometimes you may have demilitarized zones (DMZs) to secure your public-facing infrastructure from your core environment that do not have access to Active Directory. You may have multiple Active Directory domains, which means you have to keep track of multiple Domain Group Policies. Last but not least, with seemingly thousands of settings, its often hard to determine whether Group Policy, as it applies to your servers, is really configured the way you want.

That's where a great configuration management tool comes in, and Puppet Enterprise, coupled with Windows-specific modules, helps you not only automate security settings, but also validate they are correct across your systems.
